## Our Commitment

This blog is a static, read-only resource. I do not collect, store, or process personal data beyond what is strictly necessary for the technical operation of the website.

## Data We Process

### Hosting & Infrastructure

This site is hosted on a third-party platform (e.g., Vercel, Netlify, or similar). These providers may log standard server access data — including IP addresses, browser user agents, and timestamps — for security, performance monitoring, and abuse prevention. These logs are retained for a limited period and are not accessible to me for individual user tracking.

### Analytics

If analytics are enabled, we use a privacy-focused solution that does not use cookies and does not track individuals across sites. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.

### Search

The on-site search is powered by [Pagefind](https://pagefind.app/), a static search library that runs entirely in your browser. No search queries are sent to external servers.

## Cookies

This website does **not** use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party cookies. We may use a single functional cookie to remember your light/dark theme preference (`localStorage`), which is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to any server.
